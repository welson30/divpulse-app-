Frequently asked questions

How do push notifications work on iPhone?
Open DivPulse in Safari → tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen" → open from your home screen → tap Allow. 4 taps, done once. Notifications arrive on your lock screen exactly like a banking app.
Which brokers are supported?
All of them. You enter holdings manually (ticker + shares + broker name). Fidelity, Schwab, Robinhood, IBKR, XP Investimentos, Avenue, Nomad, Vanguard, Webull, Moomoo or any name you type. Automatic broker sync coming in Pro+.
How does DivPulse know my dividend dates?
We pull ex-dividend dates, payment dates and per-share amounts daily from official financial APIs. Confirmed dividends show in green, estimated dividends show in amber.
Can I see values in BRL or MXN?
Yes. Use the USD · BRL · MXN switcher in the top bar. Exchange rates update daily. Perfect for Brazilian investors using Avenue, Nomad or XP Investimentos.
Is my financial data safe?
DivPulse is a tracking tool only. We never access your broker account, never execute trades, and never hold your money. You enter tickers and share counts manually. All data is encrypted.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, cancel anytime from Settings. Your plan stays active until end of billing period. Annual plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee.
How does broker auto-sync work?
Available on Pro+. For US brokers (Fidelity, Schwab), we use Plaid — a secure industry standard used by Mint and Personal Capital. You authorize read-only access once and your holdings sync automatically. For Brazilian brokers (XP, Avenue, Nomad), auto-sync works via CSV import. Manual entry remains available on all plans.
Does DivPulse work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. DivPulse is a web app that works on any browser. On iPhone: open in Safari → tap Share → "Add to Home Screen" → open from your home screen → allow notifications. On Android: open in Chrome → tap menu → "Add to Home Screen". It installs like a native app with full notification support.
